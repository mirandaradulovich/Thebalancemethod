About Miri

A practice built on presence

Hey, I'm Miri. I'm a Yoga Therapist, Pilates Instructor, Intuitive Eating Counsellor, Psychology of Eating Coach, and Breathwork Facilitator — and more importantly, a life lover and proud mum to my beautiful girl, Summer Indigo.

My yoga journey began at 15 when a kind stranger handed me a class pass at the café I worked at in Cottesloe. I walked in with a sore body and a complicated relationship with food, and I never left. I was home.

A few years later I packed up, sold my belongings, and moved to Bali solo at 20 — immersing myself in yoga, meditation, food, and life. I trained as a certified teacher in Indonesia and made lifelong friends from around the world.

Yoga, to me, has very little to do with the shapes you make. It's about arriving on your mat and truly meeting yourself — witnessing your thoughts, coming back into your body, and remembering who you are.

I bring this same philosophy to everything I teach. Whether it's a sweaty flow to music, a Pilates reformer session, a breathwork circle, or a conversation about your relationship with food — the intention is always the same: a non-judgmental, caring, and challenging space that gets you to where you want to be.

The practices I teach are the practices that carried me through my own hard times. They work — if you're willing to do the work.

Much love, Miri ✨
