About Miranda

A practice built on presence

I'm Miranda — a certified yoga and Pilates instructor with over a decade of teaching experience across four continents. My retreats are small, intentional, and deeply personal.

I believe transformation happens not through intensity, but through consistency, breath, and genuine care. Every retreat I lead is designed to meet you exactly where you are.

Whether you're returning to movement after a long pause, or deepening an existing practice, you'll leave with more than you arrived with.
